About SRCN

SRCN (Students Research Council Nepal) Is A Non-Profit Youth Organization Dedicated To Students From High School Of Nepal, Where Young Enthusiasts From Different Fields Work On A Single Platform. The Organization Will Help To Connect Like-Minded Students And Work In Their Respective Fields Of Interest: Physics, Computer Science, Engineering, Biology, Economics, And Politics To Tackle Real World Problems Through Research.

For The Application Process. All The Students From 9-12 Are Invited To Fill Out The Application Form And Be A Part Of Our Community. Participants And The Founding Members Of SRCN Will Work Together To Complete The Projects And Research For A Certain Period Of Time.
